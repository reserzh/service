Offline-First Architecture

Works Without Signal

Basements, rural properties, metal buildings — techs lose signal constantly. FieldService Pro keeps working regardless.

Offline Mutation Queue

Status changes, notes, photos, and checklist updates are queued locally and automatically synced when connectivity returns.

Automatic Sync

The sync processor watches for connectivity changes and processes the queue with retry logic and exponential backoff.

Cached Data

TanStack Query persists job data to device storage. Techs can view job details, customer info, and schedules even offline.

Offline Photo Storage

Photos are saved to the device filesystem and queued for upload. No photos are ever lost, even in zero-signal areas.

Offline Banner

A persistent banner shows pending changes count: "Offline — 3 changes pending." Techs always know their sync status.

Optimistic Updates

UI updates instantly even offline. Checklist toggles, status changes, and notes appear immediately while queued in the background.

Route Awareness

Smart Schedule with Gap Detection

Day and week calendar views with two display styles — list and timeline. Smart gap detection highlights scheduling inefficiencies.

  • Day view with list or timeline display modes
  • Week view with day selector and job count indicators
  • Gap warnings between jobs (e.g., "2h 30m gap")
  • Current time indicator on timeline view
  • Long-press quick preview with Call and Navigate
  • Distance and travel time badges per job

Built for Real-World Field Work

Every feature was designed with input from technicians who use the app in the field every day.

Voice-to-Text Notes

Tap the mic button to dictate notes hands-free. Text is appended to the note field — no typing needed in dirty or wet conditions.

Photo Capture

Before, after, and general photos with optional captions. Stored offline if no signal, uploaded automatically when reconnected.

Digital Signatures

Capture customer signatures on the device screen. Stored with timestamps and user attribution for compliance.

Biometric Lock

Face ID and Touch ID for quick unlock. Automatic session timeout locks the app after inactivity to protect customer data.

Navigation Integration

One-tap navigation to job sites via Apple Maps, Google Maps, or Waze. Configurable map preference in settings.

Estimates & Invoices

Create multi-option estimates and view invoices on-site. Record payments in the field — cash, check, or card.
