FieldService Pro — Mobile Design Concepts

5 visual directions for the mobile technician app

Concept 1
Forge
Industrial / Brutalist

Sharp corners, monospace headings, dense layout. Deep navy with red accents. Flat cards with left accent borders.

Sharp 4px corners · Monospace type · Dense · Icon-only tab bar
Concept 2
Signal
High-Visibility Safety

Extra-bold typography, safety orange, oversized tap targets. Optimized for bright sunlight readability.

12px corners · Extra-bold 800 · Large targets · Orange circle tabs
Concept 3
Terra
Natural / Grounded

Forest green with warm gold accents. Capsule buttons, floating tab bar, organic shapes. Premium earthy feel.

20px corners · Capsule buttons · Floating tab bar · Warm shadows
Concept 4
Pulse
Dynamic / Energetic

Indigo-cyan gradients, glass-morphism in dark mode, progress rings and bars. Kinetic energy throughout.

16px corners · Gradients · Glass-morphism · Frosted tab bar
Concept 5
Mono
Minimal Typographic

Near-monochrome Swiss design. No card chrome, text-only tab bar, strict typographic scale. Maximum restraint.

0px corners · Light 300 headings · No cards · Text-only tabs
